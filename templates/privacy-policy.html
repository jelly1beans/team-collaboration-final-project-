Privacy Policy

Eye Clinic, your privacy and the security of your personal information are very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when booking appointments, using our services, or visiting our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our online booking system or contact us, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal identification information: Full name, contact number, email address
  • Health-related information: Service requested, doctor preference, and any notes you provide
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, and access times

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Schedule, confirm, and manage your appointments
  • Contact you regarding your consultation or follow-up
  • Provide tele-optometry services if applicable
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. Your information may only be shared with:

  • Authorized medical staff for appointment scheduling and patient care
  • Service providers who assist in the operation of our online booking platform
  • Government or regulatory authorities if required by law

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel only.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal information
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your data
  • Withdraw your consent for data collection at any time (note that this may affect your ability to book appointments online)

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather statistical information. This data is anonymized and used for website optimization only.

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website. By continuing to use our services, you agree to the updated terms.
